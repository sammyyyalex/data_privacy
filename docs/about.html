Team Project: Online Marketing and Data Privacy

You are to work on this project in a team of 2.

The objective of this project is to create a website that explains how online data is collected, how it can be used, and what you can do to remain anonymous online. You need to include a section in the website about why you should care and you can include your own thoughts in this section. You need to create a website with around 10 pages

You need to design the visuals and write in a style that would be appealing to one of the following 3 demographics:

  1. Teens
  2. College students / Young Adults
  3. Your grandparents

The website should include a carousel rotator on the home page. You are required to use bootstrap for the entire site, you will lose significant points for not doing this. Implement F-layout for the content pages, Z-layout for your homepage. Also include 5-10 images in your website and the images should be from public domain.

Required Content to Include, but you can add to this with your own research:

  • Becoming Anonymous: The Complete Guide to Maximum Security Online
  • Shoshana Zuboff on surveillance capitalism
  • United States of Secrets: Edward Snowden Part 1 and Part 2
  • Browser Fingerprinting
  • How you are tracked online
  • Facebook Dilemma Part 1 and Part 2
  • Look Alike Analysis
  • Legal Aspects of Online Privacy

Submission:

Submit the link to your Github repo and the website hosted on Github Pages Your website should contain:

  • Homepage
  • 6 Content pages on the video topic

Grading:

  1. 25 points for the appearance of the website
  2. 25 points for creating engaging content
  3. 25 Points for the technical Implementation
  4. 15 Points for having roughly the same number of commits of equal work from both team members.
  5. 10 points for correct submission
